~Requirements for Library Management System~

♫ User Registration and Management:
The system should allow users to register with their details (Name, User ID, Phone Number, Email) and store this information securely.

♫ Book Management:
The system should manage information about books, including their availability, author, and cost. It should update availability when a book is borrowed or returned.

♫ Borrowing and Due Management:
The system should track borrowing records, linking users to the books they’ve borrowed, along with the due date for returning the books.

♫ Fine Calculation:
The system should calculate fines for overdue books based on the borrowing record, update the user’s fine record, and allow staff to mark fines as paid.
